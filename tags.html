Tag Families

Common tag definitions.

Magic School

The story is set in a school with magical training or magical daily life.

Found Family

Characters become a chosen support group.

Slow Burn

The relationship develops slowly over time.

Comfort Read

The tone stays gentle and emotionally safe.

Rivals to Allies

Characters start in conflict and move toward trust.

Protective Team

Teammates cover for or defend each other.

High-pressure Week

A short stressful time frame shapes the story.

Late-night Confession

A key emotional scene happens late at night.

Epistolary

The story uses letters, notes, or messages.

Soft Politics

There is court or power tension, but the tone stays less harsh.
