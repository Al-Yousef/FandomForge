Definitions

Plain-language explanations for the tags used in this prototype.

Slow Burn

The relationship builds gradually across many scenes instead of paying off immediately.

See it on a story page

Found Family

A group becomes emotionally close enough to feel like chosen family.

Magic School

The story uses school routines, classes, or campus spaces as a main part of its appeal.

Team Rivalry

Characters compete or clash inside a shared team context, which keeps the stakes relational.

Protective Dynamic

Earned Confession
