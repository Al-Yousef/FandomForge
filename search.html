Helpful Filters

These are the easiest filters to understand first.

Rating

Start with General or Teen before you touch anything more detailed.

Content notes

Look for warnings written in full on the card, not hidden behind abbreviations.

Length

Use shorter reads first if you want a lower-commitment starting point.

Intensity

Soft, tense, and high-pressure stories should feel clearly different before you click.

Examples

Try writing searches like this.

Warm fantasy school

Try: "warm fantasy school story with close friendships and a slow romance."

Quiet court drama

Try: "quiet court drama with letters, tension, and a slow relationship build."

Rival team story

Try: "rival teammates under pressure who slowly start trusting each other."
