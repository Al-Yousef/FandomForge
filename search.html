Search Help

Examples of beginner-friendly search language.

Theme words

Use mood, trope, or world words like gentle, school, found family, or letters.

Dynamic words

Use relationship words like rivals to allies, protective team, or late-night confession.

Safety words

Use lighter, no graphic violence, or visible content notes when comfort matters most.
